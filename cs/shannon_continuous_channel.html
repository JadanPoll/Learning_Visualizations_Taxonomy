Continuous Channel (Sec. 24-25)

Channel Parameters

Adjust the physical constraints of the communication medium.
Bandwidth (W) 3000 Hz
Frequency range of the channel. Increases the number of independent samples per second (2W).
Signal Power (P) 1000 W
Average power of the transmitted signal.
Noise Power (N) 100 W
Average power of the white thermal noise.

Theorem 17 (Shannon-Hartley)

The maximum rate C at which information can be reliably transmitted.
C = W · log2(1 + P/N)
C = 3000 · log2(1 + 1000/100)
C = 3000 · log2(11.00)
C = 10378.2 bits/sec
Signal-to-Noise Ratio (SNR) 10.00 dB
Often expressed in decibels: 10 · log10(P/N)
