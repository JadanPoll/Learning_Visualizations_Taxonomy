Sampling Theorem (Sec. 19)

Perfect Reconstruction

Shannon proved that a band-limited continuous signal is completely determined by a set of discrete samples spaced exactly 1 / 2W seconds apart.
f(t) = Σ Xn · sinc(2Wt - n)

sinc(x) = sin(πx) / (πx)
