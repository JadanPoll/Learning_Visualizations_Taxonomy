The Dirac Sea (Eq. 1.1 - 1.3)

Hole Theory Formalism

Dirac's equation admits solutions with both positive and negative kinetic energies.
ih ∂ψ/∂t = H0ψ

Positive State: E ≥ mc²
Negative State: E ≤ -mc²
Furry and Oppenheimer formalized that the vacuum is a completely filled sea of negative energy states. An absence of an electron in this sea (a hole) is observed as a positive electron (positron).

Interactive Experiment

Fire a high-energy gamma ray photon (E > 2mc²) into the vacuum. It will promote a negative-energy electron to a positive energy state, creating an observable electron-positron pair.
